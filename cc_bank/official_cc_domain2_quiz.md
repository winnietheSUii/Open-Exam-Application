## Question 1

**What term is sometimes used interchangeably with "incident management"?**

- Business continuity

- Crisis response
- Safety management

**Explanation:** Some organizations use the term "crisis management" to describe the incident management process.

## Question 2

**What is the purpose of a red book in the context of business continuity?**

- To be distributed to all employees
- To outline procedures for routine tasks
- To provide a backup copy of the business continuity plan
- To serve as a hard copy backup accessible outside the facility

**Answer:** To serve as a hard copy backup accessible outside the facility

**Explanation:** A red book is a physical, offline copy stored outside the facility to ensure access when electronic systems or buildings are unavailable.

## Question 7

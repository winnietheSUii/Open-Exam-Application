## Question 1

**In the context of security awareness and training programs, what is the PRIMARY goal?**

- Ensuring data confidentiality
- Maximizing business capabilities
- Protecting health and human safety
- Protecting organizational assets

**Answer:** Protecting health and human safety

**Explanation:** The primary goal of security awareness and training programs is to protect the health and safety of individuals by promoting behaviors that reduce risks and prevent harm. Effective training empowers employees to recognize and respond to threats in ways that maintain a safe work environment, making human safety the foundation of all security efforts.

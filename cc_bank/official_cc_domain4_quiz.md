## Question 7

**What core cybersecurity principle does microsegmentation help enforce across different parts of an organization?**

- Data Redundancy
- Least privilege
- Physical isolation
- Rule complexity

**Answer:** Least privilege

**Explanation:** Microsegmentation supports the principle of least privilege by dividing networks into smaller segments and tightly controlling communication between them. This ensures that users, systems, or applications within one department or unit can only access the resources that are essential for their role. By minimizing unnecessary access, microsegmentation helps reduce the attack surface and limit lateral movement in the event of a breach.
